How to Play

Level 1

As the page loads the game is loaded, the player is asked what is the Capital of a Country.

Four possible answer options are dispalyed, by clicking on one the user has chosen the answer.

If the answer is correct the player recieves a point. If it is incorrect a pint is lost and a life is lost after losing four lives the game is over.

Level 2

Level 2 asks the player the same quesiton as level one. The player must fill in the answer in the input box provided. The score and lives work in the same way as they work in Level 1

To close the instructions click on the button above.
